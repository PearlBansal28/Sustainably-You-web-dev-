Eco-friendly Tips

  1. Install a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling.
  2. Collect rainwater for gardening.
  3. Practice the 3 Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.
  4. Grow your own vegetables and herbs.
  5. Drive fuel-efficient or electric vehicles.
  6. Choose direct flights to reduce your carbon footprint.
  7. Consider eco-friendly accommodations and travel options.
  8. Support sustainable and ethical brands.
  9. Invest in quality items that last longer.
  10. Declutter and only keep what you truly need.
  11. Borrow, rent, or share items instead of buying new ones.
  12. Choose clothes made from organic or recycled materials.
  13. Avoid products with harmful chemicals.
